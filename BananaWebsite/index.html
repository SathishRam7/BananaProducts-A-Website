About us

Inspired by the fusion concept of agriculture and technology, TASS is a successful startup that is blooming in its journey of educating and guiding farmers from the choice of soil to the harvest of bananas, buying them fully from the farmers and also adding value to it in later stages. A startup owned by professionals, TASS has established its market presence in 2022. We specialise in waste management of bananas by adding value to the ripened ones which are usually thrown away by integrating making them into chips, candies, cookies, pickles, jam/sauce varieties, banana leaf dip-tea and wine and much more! The other parts of the crop like fibre, leaf and stem are integrated cosmetics, sanitary pads, diapers, dyes, agarbattis, clothes, briquettes, papers, artificial hair and band-aids. With a vast knowledge of being into retailing of bananas for over a generation, TASS has made its way into the market as a remarkable brand. Our services and value-added products are handcrafted to meet the needs of every client and the demands of the market.

Vision


To create a world- class marketplace for Indian varieties of produced bananas and its value-added by products. We produce world-class export quality bananas in genuine methods.



Mission


To procure a place for Indian varieties of export- quality bananas and its value- added by products in international marketspace with professional guidance starting from farmers to market agents and simultaneously creating employment opportunities in India.



Who We Are !


TASS efficiently manages to produce world-class export quality bananas in genuine methods. Unlike other brands in the market, we don't spray propylene to fasten the ripening process, instead we use the smoke of banana crop waste. The other parts of the banana tree which have meagre market value, like fibre, leaves, and stem are handled effectively by eliminating the risk of wasting. We add value to it and integrate them into cosmetics, sanitary pads, diapers, dyes, agarbattis, clothes, briquettes, papers, artificial hair and band-aids. Also the value addition is done to ripened bananas which helps in extending its shelf life by making them into chips, candies, cookies, pickles, jam/sauce varieties, banana leaf dip-tea and wine etc..
